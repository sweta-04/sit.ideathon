SIT-Ideathon 3.0

SIT Ideathon aims to provide a platform for students to showcase their creativity, critical thinking, and collaborative skills.Through this platform, students can make their dreams come true by pursuing their entrepreneurial dreams in their future life. The competition was divided into two parts, one for school students of Class - IX to XII and the other for UG and PG students.
