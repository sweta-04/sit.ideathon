Presentation Details


Every team participating in the event is required to submit a presentation along with the registration form. The presentation should focus on their chosen topic and adhere to the following guidelines:

Presentation Content:
  1. Teams should cover key aspects of their topic, including problem statements, proposed solutions, methodology, and expected outcomes.
  2. Ensure clarity and coherence in presenting ideas.
Slide Limit:
  1. The presentation should not exceed a maximum of 8 slides
  2. Conciseness and relevance are encouraged.
File Format
  1. Submit the presentation in PDF format (Max 10 MB) only.
Submission Details:
  1. The deadline for submission is 25th January, 2025.
  2. Late submissions will not be considered.


Shortlisting Criteria


Evaluation Criteria:
  1. Presentations will be evaluated based on content quality, clarity, and adherence to the specified guidelines.
  2. Teams failing to submit the required presentation will not be eligible for participation.
Selection Details:
  1. Rigorous selection round after the registration period through their submitted presentation.
  2. Selected teams receive notification emails for final registration, submission details, and payment of a nominal registration fee of Rs 500 per team.
  3. Submit the presentation in PDF format (Max 10 MB) only.
Prize Details:
  1. Attractive Prize money for the top 3 teams.
  2. Participation e-certificate will be provided to all.
