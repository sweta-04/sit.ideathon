FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIT Ideathon about?

SIT Ideathon provides a platform for students to showcase their creativity and critical thinking by solving real-world challenges and competing with peers from nearby institutions.

Who can participate in the event?

The event is open to school students from Class XI to XII and UG/PG students from various backgrounds interested in innovation and problem-solving.

Is there any registration fee?

No, participation is completely free for all interested students and institutions. However if your idea is selected for presentation, there is a charge of Rs. 500 per team.

When does the event take place?

The event will be held on 20th January 2026, at the J.C Bose Seminar Hall, SIT Campus, Sukna, Siliguri.

What is the procedure to register?

Click the Register button, complete the Google Form with the required details, and submit your idea presentation. If selected, our representative will contact you directly.
